About Me

A student of medicine & life

I'm Christy Sajan, a third-year MBBS student at Christian Medical College, Ludhiana with a deep passion for understanding the human body and helping people. Medicine, to me, is not just a career — it's a calling.

Currently immersed in clinical postings across various departments, I'm learning to bridge the gap between textbook knowledge and real-world patient interactions. I'm particularly drawn to internal medicine and public health.

Outside academics, I volunteer at community health camps, participate in medical conferences, and enjoy reading research articles on emerging healthcare trends. I'm fluent in Hindi, English, and Malayalam.
